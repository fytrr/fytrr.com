RACE FORMAT

RACE

Choose your level — Beginner, Intermediate, or Elite — and experience the perfect mix of challenge, fun, and adventure!

At FYTRR, we stand for true equality. We don't separate participants by gender because we believe every individual—male or female—has their own unique strength and potential. Our race is designed to celebrate capability and endurance, ensuring everyone competes on equal ground based on their fitness level, not gender.

CATEGORY

  1. SINGLE
  2. MIXED

In the Mixed Category, the challenge reaches another level: participants must run or walk during Run 4 while carrying their partner, whether male or female, symbolising strength, teamwork, and unity.

RUN AND ZONE HOW IT WORK ?

The FYTRR race combines running and workouts in a progressive challenge. It starts with Run 1, followed by three workout zones — Zone 1 (2 workouts), Zone 2 (3 workouts), and Zone 3 (4 workouts) — each separated by runs. The race ends with Run 4, pushing you to the finish line and marking your ultimate victory.

ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

We are a passionate community of fitness enthusiasts, athletes, and trainers dedicated to creating India's most challenging and rewarding fitness event. The FYTRR is more than just a competition, it's a movement that celebrates strength, endurance, discipline, and the warrior spirit within everyone.

WHAT WE DO

The FYTRR brings together athletes of all levels – beginners, intermediates, and elites – to test their limits across a series of functional fitness stations. Each event is designed to push participants physically and mentally through running, strength, endurance, and power challenges. With a structured and professional setup, we ensure a safe, competitive, and unforgettable fitness experience.

Our events combine:

  • Endurance challenges (running, battle rope, burpees)
  • Strength tests (sled push, pull, tire flips, sandbag lunges)
  • Core activation (functional stability drills)
  • Explosive power moves (wall ball slams)

Our Motto

“Fight. Conquer. Repeat.”

We believe fitness is not just about building the body, but about building character, resilience, and legacy. Our motto inspires every participant to fight through their limits, conquer their fears, and repeat the journey toward a stronger, better self.
